►About  \  Tools for people learning to read Chinese.

Reading Chinese presents unique challenges - you might know an individual character, but combined with others it's gobbledegook; maybe you know a word when spoken, but haven't learned the character; or maybe you know reading will help you learn more, but it's just too tiring!

We make reading Chinese easier through:

  • Beautifully formatted text
  • Integrated pinyin and dictionary tools
  • Convert difficult Chinese webpages into easily read texts »
  • Personalised vocabulary lists »
  • Personal reading lists »

This site is in the early stages and we hope to add many more features in future:

  • Mobile applications
  • Recommended reading appropriate to your level
