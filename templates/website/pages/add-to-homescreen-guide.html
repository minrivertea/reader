Add {{ sitename }} to your homescreen

You can add this site to your mobile desktop to access it just like an app.

Visit the website in your browser and click the 'Share' icon at the bottom (the box with the upwards arrow).

Click "Add to Home Screen" from the final menu on the page.

Give the icon a name (or just use the default) and click "Add" in the top right corner.

You should now have an 'app' icon on your desktop now. When you click it, it will open the website in your default browser.
