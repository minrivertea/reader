Welcome Beta Tester!

We're going to try make learning Chinese vocabulary easier for you. Thanks for taking part - here's a little introduction to this whole thing (2 minutes tops).

This is a Chinese-English dictionary that helps you learn vocabulary faster and with less effort.

1. You use it like any other dictionary - search for words using Chinese, pinyin or English.

2. The tool remembers the words you've searched for, and sends you emails when you need to test/review something.

3. Using Spaced Repetition techniques, we optimise and automate your learning so you put in minimum effort with maximum results.

For the next month, we want you to use this as your main Chinese-English dictionary.

It's completely free and hopefully will help improve your Chinese vocabulary.

Because this is a very early test, you'll definitely bump into bugs, errors or some not-perfect features! Please try your best to stick with it and email us at info@chinesedictionary.io or use the Weixin (WeChat) group to give feedback.
