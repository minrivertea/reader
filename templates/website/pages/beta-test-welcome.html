This is a Chinese-English dictionary that helps you learn vocabulary faster and with less effort.

1. You use it like any other dictionary - search for words using Chinese, pinyin or English.

2. The tool remembers the words you've searched for, and sends you emails when you need to test/review something.

3. Using Spaced Repetition techniques, we optimise and automate your learning so you put in minimum effort with maximum results.

For the period of this beta test, please try use this dictionary as your main Chinese-English dictionary for 1 month.

It's a very early test, so you'll definitely bump into bugs, errors or features that aren't particularly great at the moment! Please try your best to stick with it and email us at info@chinesedictionary.io or use the Weixin (WeChat) group to give feedback.
