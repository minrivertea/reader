Here's some basic information on this beta test and how to use the tool.

Please read the info below (2 minutes) and then start by signing up

This is a Chinese-English dictionary that actively helps you learn vocabulary faster and with less effort.

1. You use it like any other dictionary - search for words using Chinese, pinyin or English.

2. The tool remembers the words you've searched for, and sets up automatic reviews / tests.

3. We use Spaced Repetition techniques to make sure the memorisation process is optimised.

For this beta test, please try use this dictionary as your main Chinese-English dictionary.

You'll receive email notifications when you've got vocabulary to review or test - please try hard to open those emails and do the tests/reviews, just for the period of this beta-test.

You will definitely bump into some bugs or find that the dictionary/tools are not perfect. Again, we're very thankful for your patience and please stick with it! It's a very early version, so we're trying to identify what's good and bad and what to improve.

Please give us feedback! Bugs, areas to improve, things that work well and suggestions.

Email info@chinesedictionary.io or use the Weixin group to post comments and questions.
