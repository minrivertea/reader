How often do you look up a word in a dictionary, and then forget it again two weeks later?

This site offers personal wordlists based on words you've looked up previously. Every time you search, we add that word to your list, and then over time you build up a wordlist that's focussed on you and your interests (and not a random textbook).

You have to be logged in to take advantage of personal wordlists - remember it's free to register and we'll never use your email address for spam.
