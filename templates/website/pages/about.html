About {{ sitename }}

Reading Chinese (Mandarin) presents unique challenges - we want to make it easier.

This site is a simple dictionary that (hopefully) works exceptionally fast. It's in the early stages of development, and only works as a Chinese-English dictionary. If you have any questions or problems, send them to info@chinesedictionary.io

Attributions

This site makes use of several valuable open-source tools.

  • Dictionary resources: The brilliant CEDICT is the Chinese-English dictionary source.
  • Language processing: cjklib helps keep a lid on all character inputs, outputs and filtering.
  • Technology stack: Django, Redis, Nginx, Jquery and Pjax provide all the awesome technical capabilities.
