Dictionaries suck.

How many times have you looked up a word, forgotten it, and then kicked yourself for not studying hard enough? But it's not your fault. Blame that lazy, part-time, good-for-nothing dictionary you bought.

You need a dictionary that pro-actively helps you remember, recall and learn vocabulary effectively. You need optimally timed reminders that caress, cosset and cajole you into remembering words before you forget them forever. You needa a tool that pays attention to what you search for and focuses on your needs. You need to say goodbye to:

  • Random flashcards or sets of vocabulary that you don't want, need or use.
  • A short spurt of good intentions, and then everything falling to pieces after 1 week.
  • Looking up really useful words during learning, and then losing track of them a day later
  • Archaic methods that hold back your learning

How does this (completely free) dictionary work?

Like any other online dictionary - you enter a word in Chinese, English or pinyin, and it spits out an answer. But, behind the scenes, it keeps track of your searches, and slowly builds up a history of words that are important to you, words that you wanted to learn.

Then we send you emails that help you recall and relearn those words at intervals designed for optimal learning performance. It's simple. Oh yeah, and completely free.

Privacy concerns, worries, looking for the secret catch?

If you're still not convinced, here's answers to some of your questions:

  • Yes, seriously, 100% free.
  • We won't ever, ever, ever send you spam emails. You're email address will only ever be used to send you targeted vocabulary reminders. You can control your email settings completely from your account page.
  • There's no huge, upfront effort needed by you - just use the dictionary like any other online dictionary, and over time we will help you learn more vocabulary by being active about your learning.
