Get started

It's a Chinese dictionary - search for words and it will give you back meanings, pronunciations and examples. It's completely free to use.

Our system automatically saves your searches, and then schedules times for vocab reviews or tests using spaced repetition algorithms.

Registered users will get notifications (by email) whenever a word is ready to be reviewed or tested.

How it works

Your memory is like a rubber-band - pull it too much and it will snap.
Pull it too little and it will never stretch.

How many times have you looked up a word in the dictionary, and then had to look it up again a week later? To learn vocabulary effectively, you need to be reminded and tested at exactly the right time - just as you're about to forget.
