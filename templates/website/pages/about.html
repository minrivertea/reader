Get Started

This dictionary is completely free to use.
Type a Chinese, English or pinyin word into the search box above and hit 'Enter' to start. When you're ready, signup for an account and you'll start getting optimised notifications that proactively help you learn Chinese vocabulary.

How it works

1.
You search for words and get answers
Lookup words using English, Chinese or pinyin and you'll get character definitions, word examples, pronunciation guides and more, just like you'd expect from a standard Chinese dictionary.

2.
We schedule reviews and tests
We build up your personal wordlist, and based on spaced repetition algorithms and adjusting for your individual abilities, we schedule reviews and tests at exactly the right time so you do as little study as possible, but also remember as much as possible.

3.
You get notified when you need to test something.
Whenever you need to review or test a vocabulary item, we email you. You click on a link, take a very, very short test and then that's it. You spend the minimum amount of time learning, and absolutely zero time writing flashcards, scheduling your learning, copying from notebooks...

Your memory is like a rubber-band - pull it too much and it will snap.
Pull it too little and it will never stretch.

Effective memorisation is a simple technique, but we typically pick up terrible habits at school - cramming for tests, huge piles of flashcards or thick books, several hour study sessions...

In reality, memorisation requires that you repeatedly learn and then recall (remember) something at exactly the right time for it to bury itself deeper and deeper in your memory.

This dictionary can helps you by automatically planning when and what you should study, so that you learn vocab efficiently and don't waste precious time and effort.
