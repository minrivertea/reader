Dictionaries suck. How many times have you looked up a word, forgotten it, and then blamed yourself for not studying hard enough? No - blame that lazy, part-time, good-for-nothing dictionary you bought.

This is a simple dictionary that pro-actively helps you remember and learn new words.

  • Search and use just like a normal online dictionary
  • We keep a record of all the vocabulary you look up.
  • Then the magic starts...

Using spaced repetition techniques and a variety of learning strategies, we ensure that every time you look up a word, it becomes part of your own personal language curriculum.

Attributions and thanks

This site makes use of several valuable open-source tools.

  • Dictionary resources: The brilliant CEDICTprovided the core Chinese-English dictionary source.
  • Language processing: cjklib helps keep a lid on all character inputs, outputs and filtering.
  • Technology stack: Django, Redis, Nginx, Jquery and Pjax provide all the awesome technical capabilities. egukin provides the awesome pinyin converter which saved hours of mind-twisting.
