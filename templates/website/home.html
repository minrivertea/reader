Turn hard-to-read Chinese articles into clear, readable and pinyin-annotated text.

What does it do?

Chinese is hard to read, especially on the web, so it's doubly-difficult for a non-native speaker. This tool helps Chinese learners by formatting characters more clearly, annotating them with pinyin and definitions, and giving you extra tools that advance your learning.
