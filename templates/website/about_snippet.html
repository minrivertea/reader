This site is a set of tools for people who are learning to read Chinese.

Reading Chinese comes with unique challenges - while you might know the meaning of individual characters, it's sometimes hard to know their combined meaning; similarly, often you are blocked from learning and remembering a character because of the lack of pronunciation guides.

Our aim is to make reading Chinese easier:

  • Well formatted text
  • Pinyin guides
  • Integrated dictionary
  • Bookmarklet to convert Chinese webpages into easily formatted text

We're currently in early stages of development, and hope to add many more featuers in future:

  • Personalised reading lists
  • Mobile applications
  • Recommended reading appropriate to your level
