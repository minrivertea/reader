Convert any long Chinese web-text into beautiful, easy-to-read text.

If you read Chinese on the web, it's often difficult - small text, tightly packed, and no pinyin or dictionary! This bookmarklet solves the problem. Turn any long page of Chinese text into a well formatted document, easy to read and understand.

How to use

Bookmark Me!


Drag and drop the button above into your browser's bookmarks toolbar. Then, when you're on a long page of Chinese text, click the bookmark and it will automagically convert the webpage you are reading into something beautiful!

Try going to this page on the NYTimes Chinese website, and then clicking the bookmarklet button you've just dragged into your toolbar.
